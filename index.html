About ShapesPrison

Our Mission and Values

ShapesPrison is dedicated to upholding the highest standards of correctional management, ensuring the secure confinement of individuals while providing opportunities for rehabilitation. Our mission extends to fostering a safer community through structured programs and responsible oversight.

We believe in accountability, respect, and the potential for positive change. Our staff are trained to maintain a secure environment while supporting inmates in their journey towards reintegration.

Our Facilities

Housing Units

Modern, secure housing units designed for various security levels, ensuring safety and order. Each unit is equipped with necessary amenities and monitored around the clock.

Rehabilitation Programs

Comprehensive programs including educational courses, vocational training, and counseling services aimed at reducing recidivism and preparing individuals for successful reentry into society.

Medical & Support Services

On-site medical, dental, and mental health services provided by qualified professionals, ensuring the well-being of all inmates.
